## COVID-19 Response

Our world has shifted in a remarkable way due to the COVID-19 pandemic, and we have found that we need each other and support in a way like never before. We know that it is incredibly important for new parents to have the support they need as they welcome home a new baby, and we want to be there to help, especially now.
We are following very strict guidelines and protocols to make sure our doulas stay healthy and to keep the families and babies we are working with safe. These include limiting the number of doulas per family, following all of the guidelines set forth by the CDC, taking temperatures before going into a shift, removing shoes before entering a family's home, and wearing masks while working.

For information on virtual support, or to find out how After the Stork can help you transition to life with a newborn please [contact us](https://afterthestork.info/contact/). We would love to support you during this time!
